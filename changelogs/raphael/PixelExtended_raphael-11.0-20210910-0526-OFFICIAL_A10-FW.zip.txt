NOTE:
** clean flash is mandatory
** A10 Firmware is needed
** For this version it is not possible to do the OTA update (it will be functional in the next releases)

Installation guide:
- Back up all your data
- Flash last A10 Firmware for your region
- Flash Rom
- Flash DFE if you don't want to be encrypted, otherwise you need the format data
- reboot system
