Clean Flash
- Back up all your data
- Wipe Cache - Dalvik - System - Data - Vendor
- Flash latest firmware required by ROM
- Flash Rom
- Flash DFE if you don't want to be encrypted, otherwise you need the format data
- reboot system

Dirty Flash

- Back up all your data
- Wipe Cache - Dalvik
- Flash Rom
- Flash DFE
- reboot system
