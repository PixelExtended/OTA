#Flashing instructions

Clean Flash
- Back up all your data
- Flash Recommended Recovery
- Wipe Cache - Dalvik - System - Data - Vendor
- Flash latest firmware available for your Device region
- Flash Rom
- Flash DFE if you want to stay decypted
- reboot system

Dirty Flash
**source got rebased, clean flash is recommended!!**

- Back up all your data
- Wipe Cache - Dalvik
- Flash Rom
- Flash DFE if you want to stay decrypted
- reboot system

Enjoy the PEX
