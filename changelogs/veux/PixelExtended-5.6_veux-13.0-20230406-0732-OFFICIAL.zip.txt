- First Official release
- Can be flashed over existing unofficial build
- Made with 13.0.7.0 Global Kernel and Vendor
 
  #Flashing instructions


Clean Flash
- Back up all your data
- Flash Aosp Recovery
- Wipe Data / factory Reset 
- Flash Rom via Adb sideload or use SD Card 
- reboot system

Dirty Flash

- Back up all your data
- Reboot to Recovery 
- Flash Rom via Adb sideload or use SD Card
- reboot system

Enjoy the PEX
